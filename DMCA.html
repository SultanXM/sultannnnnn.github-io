DMCA Compliance

Reedstreams ("we," "us," or "our") respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies with the provisions of the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). This DMCA Policy outlines our procedures for handling copyright infringement claims related to our website (the "Service").

Reedstreams does not host, upload, or store any streaming content on our servers. Our Service acts solely as an aggregator of links to live sports streams hosted by third-party providers. All videos and streams accessible through our Service are hosted on external platforms that are freely available online. As such, we do not control the content, quality, or availability of these streams.

2

Submitting a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that content linked through Reedstreams infringes your copyright, we encourage you to contact the third-party hosting provider directly, as they are responsible for the content. However, if you wish to submit a DMCA takedown notice to Reedstreams to remove a link to allegedly infringing content, please provide a written notification containing the following information, as required by 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3):

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on their behalf.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed, or, if multiple works are covered, a representative list of such works.
  • Identification of the material claimed to be infringing, including the specific URL(s) of the link(s) on Reedstreams, to enable us to locate the material.
  • Your contact information, including your name, address, telephone number, and email address.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the copyright owner's behalf.
Send DMCA Notices To

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will promptly remove or disable access to the identified link(s) and notify the third-party provider, if applicable. Please note that we may not have control over the actual content hosted by third-party platforms.

3

Counter-Notification

If you believe that a link was removed or disabled as a result of a DMCA takedown notice in error, you may submit a counter-notification in accordance with 17 U.S.C. § 512(g). Your counter-notification must include:

  • Your physical or electronic signature.
  • Identification of the material that was removed or disabled, including the URL(s) where it appeared on Reedstreams.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you have a good-faith belief that the material was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification.
  • Your name, address, telephone number, and email address.
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal district court for the judicial district in which your address is located (or Delaware, USA if you are outside the U.S.), and that you will accept service of process from the person who submitted the original DMCA notice.
Send Counter-Notifications To

Please send your counter-notification to: Reedstreams000@gmail.com

Upon receiving a valid counter-notification, we may restore the removed link(s) within 10-14 business days, unless the original complainant notifies us of a court action seeking to restrain the restoration.
