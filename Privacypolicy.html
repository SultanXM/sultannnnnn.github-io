Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how ReedStreams protects your data and respects your privacy.

Last Updated: Oct 24, 2025
1

Introduction

Welcome to Reedstreams ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website (the "Service"). Reedstreams is a free, premium-free platform that aggregates links to live sports streaming content hosted by third-party providers. We do not collect cookies, personal data, or require user accounts.

2

Information We Do Not Collect

Reedstreams does not collect, store, or process any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • IP addresses, browsing history, or device information.
  • Account information, as we do not offer user accounts or subscriptions.

Our Service is designed to be completely free and accessible without requiring any registration or personal data submission.

3

Third-Party Streaming Links

Reedstreams provides links to live sports streams hosted by external third-party providers. We do not host, upload, or control any streaming content. When you access these third-party streams, the respective providers may collect data, such as IP addresses, cookies, or other tracking information, subject to their own privacy policies.

Important Notice

We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before accessing their content.

4

Free and Premium-Free Service

Reedstreams is a completely free service with no premium subscriptions, paid features, or hidden costs. We do not require users to create accounts, log in, or provide any payment information. Our goal is to provide seamless access to sports streaming links without collecting any personal or financial data.

5

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, there is no user data to secure on our end. However, we recommend using secure browsing practices, such as updated antivirus software and secure internet connections, when accessing third-party streaming sites linked through our Service.

6

Our Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under 13 has accessed our Service, please contact us at Reedstreams000@gmail.com.

Reedstreams does not display advertisements on our platform. However, third-party streaming sites linked through our Service may include advertisements that could involve tracking technologies. We have no control over these advertisements or the data they may collect. Please review the privacy policies of third-party sites for more information.

8

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9
